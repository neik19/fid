FAQ

We take trash and turn it into treasures... or at least we try! Here are some frequently asked questions to help you understand our subscription services, trust us we have heard everything. Rest assured, we've got your garbage handled—no matter which tier you choose!


What does the appraisal service include?

A: For our Trash Fanatic Package, we'll appraise up to 2 lbs of your trash—anything over that comes with a small handling fee. Garbage Pro Members can appraise up to 10 lbs without extra charges. As an Ultimate Dumpster VIP Fire+ member, you enjoy unlimited appraisals by weight, but we kindly limit you to 40 lbs per day for quality assurance.
Rest assured, our experts take pride in determining your trash's hidden value (if any).


Why are there limits on appraisal and sorting?

A:These limits ensure we can maintain the highest quality of service possible while handling everyone's trash. VIP members do enjoy fewer restrictions because, frankly, we like them better.


Why do the packages have weight limits?

A: To ensure fair use and to prevent anyone from abusing our services. Handling trash is an art, and we want to give all our customers the optimal experience.


What happens to the trash after you take it?

A: That's a great question. We process trash through a combination of recycling, selling, and mysterious internal selling methods. Let's just say it all goes to “good use.” (Definetely not being sold to other people for profit)


What is the "handling fee" mentioned in the appraisal service?

A: The handling fee covers the additional labor and effort required to evaluate heavier trash. Think of it as an investment in knowing just how much your garbage is worth.


Can I request a specific technician for pickups?

A: At this time, we do not allow technician selection. Our technicians are randomly assigned based on availability and mood. VIP members may have better luck getting a smile out of them.


What's the deal with “No Questions Asked” Garbage Storage/Disposal?

A: We don't ask; you don't tell. Our No Questions Asked disclosure ensures your trash is handled discreetly. Of course, some rules apply (details buried in our 12-page Terms & Conditions).
