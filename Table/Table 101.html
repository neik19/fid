Big Bubbler		 The Big Bubbler is a stationary barrier that protects from enemy ink. It also acts as a beacon for team members to super jump from. However, it is easy to destroy and enemy players can go inside of the barrier by just walking/swimming in.
  • "Support"
  • "Defensive"
  • "Easy to build up"
  • "Shield"
  • Gets paired with high turf output weapons
  • Fills up quickly
  • Critical for moments of high intensity during battles
  • Makes for a decent beacon point
  • It is relatively weak and can be destroyed quickly, especially in a battle with multiple players
  • Enemy players can get inside it by either walking/swimming in it, although it is a risky move, the Big Bubbler can be shred even more quickly while inside of it
  • Other Specials can go thgrough it without providing any sort of protection to the user

Booyah Bomb		 The user holds up a glowing ball of ink that charges up over time and can be thrown once fully charged to make an ink explosion. The user and their teammates can use the "Booyah!" signal to charge up faster.
  • "Offensive"
  • "Displacer"
  • "High Damage"
  • "High Health"
  • Easy to pull off
  • Recharges speacial gauge of teammates when they respond with "Booyah!" while charging up
  • Forces targeted enemy team to displace from their current location
  • High damage attack, guarantees splatting enemy if enemy is inside of blast range
  • Large blast range
  • Any sub weapons that were in the blast range get negated by blast. Also goes for sub weapons that are thrown into blast range while Booyah Bomb is active
  • While charging attack, user gets a shield of 470 HP, which only gets released by throwing the Booyah Bomb or enemy players breaking the shield
  • Generally gets paired up with weapons of low turf output
  • Fills up relatively slow
  • Has a long wind up animation when starting to do the Special before receiving shield

    Ink Vac    		 The Ink Vac is a vacuum cleaner that sucks up ink that is still airborne and fires it back as a super attack, similar to the Rainmaker shot. The more ink it absorbs, the bigger the blast
    • "Support"
    • "Backline"
    • "Offensive/Defensive"
    • "Shield"
    • Absorbs enemy ink, protecting teammates and allowing for a powerful counterattack.
    • Effective for turning defense into offense.
    • When fully charged, the blast has a decent radius and displaces enemy players
    • Requires good positioning, it is also very vulnerable to flanking.
    • Limited area of absorption.

    Ink Jet    		 The Inkjet gives its user the ability to hover in the air and shoot powerful blaster-like projectiles. Once it expires, the user automatically Super Jumps back to where they first activated it.
    • "Flanking"
    • "Offensive"
    • "High Damage"
    • "High Mobility"
    • "High Precision"
    • High mobility, allowing for aerial attacks and hard-to-reach targets.
    • Powerful shots with splash damage.
    • Able to go into squid form to throw off aim of other players.
    • Vulnerable during activation and at the return point.
    • Requires good aim for effective use.
    • Makes you a high point of priority for getting targeted
    		 A

    Kraken Royale    		 The Kraken Royale turns the player into a very large, invincible Kraken, that can splat opponents through a melee attack or a charged attack. It allows free travel through enemy ink.
    • "Offensive"
    • "Melee"
    • "Flanking"
    • "High Damage"
    • "Displacer"
    • The players becomes temporarily invulnerable, allowing you to survive enemy attacks and push into heavily defended areas.
    • The Kraken moves quickly and can climb walls, giving it great map control.
    • The Kraken's charged spin attack can splat enemies instantly, making it a formidable close-range threat.
    • Enemies are forced to scatter or retreat when the Kraken is active, disrupting their positioning and plans.
    • The Kraken's mobility, while fast, follows predictable paths. Skilled opponents can dodge or counterattack once the special ends.
    • The Kraken is a melee-focused special, so it's ineffective against distant foes or high-ground weapons with good range.
    • Its utility is reduced on maps with open spaces and fewer vertical surfaces, where wall-climbing and ambushes are harder to execute.
    		 S

    Reefslider    		 The Reefslider is an inflatable shark that the player rides on to dash forward a certain distance before exploding.
    • "Offensive"
    • "Displacer"
    • High Damage"
    • The explosion at the end of the Reefslider's charge can instantly splat enemies within its radius.
    • The dash component lets you quickly close the distance on enemies or reposition strategically.
    • Can be used as a defensive tool to escape dangerous situations by dashing out of enemy territory.
    • The dash follows a straight line, making it easy for attentive opponents to predict and avoid.
    • Vulnerable to long-range attacks or bombs while traveling.
    • After the explosion, there's a brief moment of vulnerability where you can be counterattacked if not careful.
    • The explosion's effective radius is relatively small, requiring precise positioning to maximize impact.
    • Smart opponents can bait the Reefslider and then focus fire on you before or after its activation.
    • Has a relativelylong wind down animation after use, and leaves you with barely any movement options after use
    		 D

    Tacticooler    		 The Tacticooler is a mini-fridge that when set up, contains four drinks that grant special effects for 17 seconds, these special abilities are Swim/Run Speed Up, intensity action, Quick Super Jump, Quick Respawn, Special Saver and Ink Resistance Up.
    • "Support"
    • "Provides Buffs"
    • "Offensive/Defensive"
    • Teamwide buffs
    • The respawn boost eliminates the special penalty, making it particularly useful in aggressive or objective-focused modes.
    • Promotes team synergy by rewarding players who stick together to grab the buffs.
    • Even if the Tacticooler is destroyed after deployment, teammates retain the buffs until they respawn.
    • Quick respawn allows for relentless aggression and faster regrouping after eliminations.
    • Provides no offensive or defensive capabilities beyond the buffs, leaving it vulnerable to direct attacks.
    • The buffs last only a limited time, so proper timing and positioning are crucial.
    • Deploying it in a contested or exposed area might result in its destruction before teammates can benefit.
    		 S

    Tenta Missiles    		 The Tenta Missiles allow users to lock on to opponents before firing ten to twenty missiles between the locked-on targets. This Special has no limit on range and can lock on any target in any part of the map, only limit is the amount of targets it can lock onto with its target radius that popus up on screen when activated.
    • "Offensive/Defensive"
    • "High Range"
    • "Displacer"
    • "Low Mobility"
    • Can lock onto multiple enemies (up to five) at once, spreading pressure across the battlefield.
    • Forces enemies to scatter, disrupting their positioning and strategies.
    • Does not require precise aim; simply lock onto targets and fire.
    • Can be activated from anywhere on the map, allowing you to target opponents from a safe location.
    • Highly effective in modes like Tower Control, Clam Blitz, and Rainmaker, where it forces opponents to abandon the objective temporarily.
    • Tenta Missiles rarely splat opponents directly unless they're cornered or inattentive.
    • Skilled opponents can dodge the missiles with good movement or by hiding under cover.
    • Poor positioning during deployment can result in being splatted before firing, as activating the Special locks you into a stationary animation until Special is deployed.
    		 A

    Triple Inkstrike    		 The Triple Inkstrike allows players to throw three missile beacons and fire missiles at where the beacons land, creating vortices of ink upon impact.
    • "Displacer"
    • "Mid-Range"
    • "Low Mobility"
    • Deploys three powerful ink tornadoes that can force enemies to move out of critical areas.
    • The ability to aim three separate strikes gives you precise control over where to apply pressure.
    • Can be activated from a distance, allowing you to contribute to the battle without putting yourself at immediate risk.
    • Each tornado has a relatively small radius, meaning opponents can avoid them if they're paying attention.
    • The time between activation and the strikes landing can give enemies time to evade.
    • Unlike some specials (e.g., Reefslider or Trizooka), the Triple Inkstrike doesn't immediately pressure enemies in its activation phase.
    • Needs to be timed well to catch enemies off guard.
    • Very low mobility as you cannot swim while the Special is active.
    		 C

    Trizooka    		 The Trizooka is a modified Inkzooka that fires three blasts at once and can be fired three times in total for each use.
    • "Long-Range"
    • "Offensive"
    • "High Damage"
    • "High Precision"
    • Fires three large explosive shots, each capable of splatting enemies within a wide radius.
    • Can take out multiple opponents in one shot if they're clustered.
    • Useful for zoning enemies and applying pressure in key areas.
    • Only three shots per activation, so each shot must be used wisely.
    • While firing, you're locked into a slower movement speed, making you an easier target.
    • Requires good aim and prediction to land shots, especially on moving targets.
    		 A

    Ultra Stamp    		 The Ultra Stamp is a large hammer which can be slammed repeatedly while advancing forwards and also has a jump attack. The weapon can also be thrown as a long-range attack, ending the special early.
    • "High Damage"
    • "Displacer"
    • "Offensive"
    • The stamping attack deals massive damage to opponents and objects (e.g., Rainmaker shield, Splash Walls).
    • The Ultra Stamp's swinging arc covers a broad area, making it difficult for enemies to dodge.
    • Can clear multiple enemies in clustered engagements.
    • Can be thrown as a long-range attack, creating a powerful explosion at the target location.
    • The stamping charge follows a straight path, making it easier for skilled opponents to dodge or counter.
    • The hitboxes of the Ultra Stamp are weird. While active, enemy players can still hurt you even while being targets of the main front stamping attack
    • The hitbox while throwing the hammer is weird as well, it only splats an enemy if the hammer goes above their hit box and if the hammer touches a surface, it explodes into a ink explosion that does splash damage, that barely does any damage.
    • Once you start stamping forward, it's harder to change direction or react quickly to enemy movements.
    		 D

    Wave Breaker    		 The Wave Breaker is a reconfigured badminton birdie that upon setting up, will send out wave signals across that ground that deal damage and mark opponents.
    • "Support"
    • "Defensive"
    • "Displacer"
    • "Reconnaissance"
    • Pulses emitted by the Wave Breaker highlight enemies hit, providing valuable intel to your team.
    • Excellent for revealing hidden or flanking opponents.
    • Repeated waves deal chip damage, softening up enemies for teammates to finish off.
    • Helps teammates by reducing enemy mobility and giving them openings for pushes or defensive plays.
    • The Wave Breaker is fixed in place after activation, limiting its range and requiring careful placement.
    • Skilled opponents can dodge the waves by jumping over them, reducing its effectiveness against agile players.
    • On its own, the Wave Breaker won’t splat enemies unless they repeatedly fail to dodge the waves.
    		 A

    Zipcaster    		 The Zipcaster allows players to latch onto walls from a distance and zip over to them while still being able to use their main weapon. Once it expires, the user automatically comes back to where they first activated it.
    • "Offensive"
    • "High Mobility"
    • "High Precision"
    • "Distraction"
    • Allows rapid movement across the map by zipping to walls and surfaces.
    • Catches enemies off guard by attacking from unexpected angles.
    • Effective for disrupting backliners or breaking defensive setups.
    • Forces opponents to focus on your unpredictable movement, creating openings for your team.
    • While zipping, you're not invincible, and skilled enemies can track and splat you mid-air.
    • The Zipcaster has a limited active time, meaning you must act quickly to maximize its value.
    • Enemies can anticipate where you'll return once the special ends and ambush you there.
    		 A
